\subsection*{Tutte matrix}

Given an undirected graph $G=(V, E)$, its Tutte matrix is:
\[
T_{i j} = \begin{cases} 
      x_{i j} & \text{if $i < j$ and $(i, j) \in E$} \\
      -x_{j i} & \text{if $i > j$ $(i, j) \in E$} \\
      0 & \text{otherwise.}
   \end{cases}
\]
$\det(T) \ne 0$ if and only if $G$ has a perfect matching.
