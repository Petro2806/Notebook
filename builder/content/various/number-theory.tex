\subsection{Stirling}

$S(n, k)$ --- number of ways to divide $n$ element into $k$ non-empty groups.\\

$S(n, n) = 1$, $n \ge 0$\\
$S(n, 0) = 0$, $n > 0$\\

$S(n, k) = S(n - 1, k - 1) + S(n - 1, k) * k$.


$B_n = \sum S(n, k)$ from $n = 0$:
